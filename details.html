Program Details

Organized by the Langfang Municipal Government (Langfang, Hebei Province, China) and funded by the Langfang University Alumni Foundation, a registered non-profit organization, this program offers a unique opportunity for US students to experience China firsthand. This immersive program goes beyond the typical tourist experience, delving deep into the vibrant cities of Langfang, Tianjin, and Beijing. Students will have the chance to explore iconic landmarks like the Great Wall of China and the Forbidden City and also discover hidden gems and local neighborhoods. The Langfang University Alumni Foundation, with its strong ties to the region, plays a vital role in facilitating authentic cultural interactions. Students will have the chance to connect with residents, university students, and cultural experts, fostering friendships and gaining a deeper understanding of Chinese traditions, history, and way of life. This exchange program is more than just sightseeing; it's a chance to broaden perspectives, build lasting connections, and create unforgettable memories that will bridge the gap between US and Chinese cultures.

What's Included (Provided Expenses)

  • Flight Reimbursement: We will reimburse you $1,000 towards your flight costs after the completion of the program.
  • Accommodation: Relax and enjoy your experience! All hotel stays during your time in China are covered on this tour.
  • Meals: Savor the flavors of China! We'll provide all food and drinks throughout the tour program in China.
  • Transportation: Enjoy seamless travel! All in-country transportation expenses are covered during your time in China on this tour.

What You'll Need to Cover (Required Personal Expenses)

  • Remaining Flight Cost: We'll reimburse $1,000, but you'll be responsible for any remaining flight costs not covered by the stipend.
  • Visa Application Fee: A separate fee of $140 is required for your Chinese visa application.
  • Travel Insurance: Travel insurance is mandatory for participation. Prices vary depending on your chosen plan.

Additional Expenses (Optional Personal Expenses)

  • Shopping & Souvenirs: Bring back mementos and gifts! This category covers personal spending money for shopping and souvenirs.
