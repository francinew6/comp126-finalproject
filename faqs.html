Frequently Asked Questions

  1. 1. What is the China-US Culture Exchange Tour?

    The China-US Culture Exchange Tour is a program designed to bridge the gap between China and the United States. This unique experience fosters cultural understanding and exchange between participants from both countries. Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage and vibrant contemporary life of China during this exciting tour.

  2. 2. Who can participate?

    The tour welcomes anyone from high school to college with a passion for exploring cultural exchange. Whether you're curious about similarities or fascinated by differences, this tour is for you.

  3. 3. What activities are included?

    The itinerary boasts a diverse range of activities designed to fully immerse you in Chinese culture. Expect visits to historical landmarks, museums, and art galleries. Engage in cultural performances, workshops, and interactive sessions with local communities. There might even be language exchanges, culinary experiences, and outdoor adventures. The final confirmed itinerary will be sent out after registration closes and before departure.

  4. 4. How long is the tour?

    The China-US Culture Exchange Tour is an immersive 10-day experience.

  5. 5. What are the benefits of participating?

    This tour offers a wealth of benefits. Gain a deeper understanding of Chinese culture, build lifelong friendships and professional connections, broaden your perspective, enhance your cross-cultural communication skills, and create unforgettable memories that will last a lifetime.

  6. 6. Are there experienced guides?

    Absolutely. The tour is led by experienced guides and facilitators who are experts in Chinese culture. They'll provide guidance, support, and insights throughout your journey, ensuring a smooth and enriching experience for everyone.

  7. 7. How can I prepare for the tour?

    Preparation is key. Before embarking on this adventure, familiarize yourself with Chinese culture, history, and customs. Pack appropriately for the climate and planned activities. Don't forget to obtain any necessary travel documents and vaccinations. Most importantly, come with an open mind and a respectful attitude towards different cultural perspectives. International travel insurance is mandatory for participation.

  8. 8. What level of Mandarin is required?

    No Mandarin knowledge is required. An English-speaking interpreter will accompany the group for the entire itinerary. For those interested in practicing their Chinese, there will be plenty of opportunities throughout the tour.

  9. 9. Meeting up and departing

    The organizer will arrange pick-up at the Beijing Capital International Airport (PEK) on the first day and drop-off on the last day.

  10. 10. How do I sign up?

    Information on signing up for the China-US Culture Exchange Tour, including registration procedures, fees, and deadlines, can be found at the beginning of the program information document. You'll need to complete an application form and submit any required documents or payments to secure your spot.

  11. 11. How can I get a Chinese visa?

    The visa application process involves completing an online form at https://cova.mfa.gov.cn and an in-person visit to a Chinese Visa Office to finalize the application.

  12. 12. Booking flights

    You are responsible for booking your own flights to and from the Beijing Capital International Airport (PEK). While you can choose your departure date from the US, ensure your arrival is on June 11th for the Welcome Ceremony and airport pick-up. You may also choose to stay in China for personal travel after the completion of the program; however, program-arranged airport drop-off is only available for departures on the last day of the program on June 20th.
