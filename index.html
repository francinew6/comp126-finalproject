China-US Culture Exchange Program

Beyond Borders, Beyond Expectations

Program Introduction

The China-US Culture Exchange Tour invites you to embark on a transformative journey into the heart of Chinese culture. Organized by the Langfang University Alumni Foundation, this program offers a unique opportunity to explore China's rich heritage while fostering meaningful connections between American and Chinese communities.

Explore Your Destinations

Journey through three vibrant cities, each offering unique cultural experiences and unforgettable memories.

Langfang

Nestled between the bustling capital of Beijing and the dynamic port city of Tianjin, Langfang offers a captivating glimpse into modern China. This city of 5.6 million thrives on a unique blend of rich cultural heritage and cutting-edge advancements. While awe-inspiring landmarks like the Grand Epoch City draw visitors, Langfang also boasts serene nature parks and hidden treasures like the art of willow-knitting handicrafts and rejuvenating geothermal hot springs. Langfang promises an experience that goes beyond the typical tourist itinerary, offering a chance to delve into the city's distinctive character and the spirit of contemporary China.

Activities & Excursions:

  • Cultural orientation and language workshops
  • Visit the Langfang Silk Road Center and the Global Arts and Culture Center
  • Interaction with Langfang University students
  • Traditional Chinese calligraphy and art classes
  • Visit the Unique Dream of Red Mansion theatrical complex

Tianjin

Tianjin, a bustling metropolis on China's eastern coast, seamlessly blends its rich history with modern innovation. This powerhouse port city, boasting a population of 15.6 million, is a vibrant hub where the past and future collide. Immerse yourself in culture-rich landmarks like the iconic Tianta Tower, a symbol of the city's progress. For a touch of whimsy, explore the visually stunning Tianjin Binhai Library, a haven for bookworms and a favorite on social media. Tianjin's commitment to international exchange is evident at its renowned university focused on foreign languages and cultural studies. And when hunger strikes, delve into the city's culinary scene, where world-famous delicacies like Goubuli Buns tantalize your taste buds. Tianjin offers an unforgettable experience, where history whispers from ancient structures and innovation pulsates through the modern streets.

Activities & Excursions:

  • Explore the historic Five Great Avenues with European-style architecture
  • Visit the Ancient Culture Street for traditional crafts and snacks
  • Tour the iconic Tianjin Eye Ferris Wheel
  • Experience the Haihe River cruise
  • Sample famous Tianjin cuisine

Beijing

Immerse yourself in the heart of China at Beijing, a majestic metropolis teeming with 22.1 million people. This dynamic capital city boasts iconic landmarks that ignite the imagination. Marvel at the Great Wall's serpentine grandeur, a testament to China's rich history. Explore the Forbidden City's opulent palaces, once the seat of imperial power. Seek tranquility within the serene gardens of the Summer Palace. But Beijing's magic extends far beyond these renowned attractions. Venture into the labyrinthine alleyways, where hidden gems unfold. Savor delectable cuisine at traditional eateries and discover historical treasures tucked away from the tourist throngs. Beijing's vibrant art scene pulsates with creativity, offering a glimpse into the soul of the city.. Beijing promises an unforgettable journey, where ancient history collides with modern energy, and cultural experiences linger long after your visit.

Activities & Excursions:

  • Walk along the magnificent Great Wall of China
  • Explore the Forbidden City
  • Experience traditional Beijing hutong neighborhoods
  • Shop at local markets and modern shopping districts

Note: Detailed itinerary and additional activities will be provided upon program acceptance.
